Hair Transplantation

Advanced hair transplantation techniques like FUE, Micro FUE, and Bio FUE are combined with personalised treatment procedures for rich and natural-looking hair.

Eyebrow Transplantation

We fully recreate an eyebrow using donor hair. An eyebrow transplant is a procedure similar to a hair transplant, and works to reconstruct your eyebrows.

Beard Transplantation

Achieve a thick and natural beard with our beard transplants, featuring full control on the depth and the direction of the implanted hair.

Moustache Transplantation

Get a thick and natural-looking moustache that is completely customised according to your requirement and pattern of hair growth.

Mesotherapy Treatment

A non-surgical alternative for hair regrowth that provides the scalp with a boost of vitamins for improving blood flow and treating bald patches.

Micropigmentation Treatment

This cosmetic procedure is used to improve or replace lost skin colouring, as well as to enhance eyebrows, eyelashes, and lips.
